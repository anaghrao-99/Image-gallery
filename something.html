Download eAadhar Card

  1. Please open UIDAI Aadhar download page by clicking here.
  2. Then select Aadhar instead of Enrolment Id near Reset button.
  3. Now enter your Aadhar number, Full name, Pincode and Security code in the box.
  4. Click on Get One Time Password button.
  5. You will receive an OTP on your registered mobile.
  6. Please enter OTP in the box and click on Validate & Download button.
  7. Your Aadhar Card will be downloaded on your computer.
